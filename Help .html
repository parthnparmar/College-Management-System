How to Use the Student Results Page

Welcome to the Student Results page of the College Management System. This page allows you to view and download the results of students based on their department and semester. Hereâ€™s how to use it:

  • Step 1: Select a department from the "Department" dropdown. The available options include:
    • Computer Science
    • Digital Electronics
    • Electrical Engineering
    • Mechanical Engineering
  • Step 2: Choose the semester from the "Semester" dropdown. You can choose between Semester 1, Semester 2, and Semester 3.
  • Step 3: Once the department and semester are selected, the student results for that department and semester will be displayed in the table below.
  • Step 4: To download the results in CSV format, click the "Download CSV" button below the table. This will generate a CSV file with the student data that you can save to your computer.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • Q: How do I filter the results by department and semester?
  • A: Simply use the "Department" and "Semester" dropdowns at the top of the page to filter the results based on your preferences.
  • Q: How can I download the student results?
  • A: Click the "Download CSV" button, and the student results will be downloaded as a CSV file. You can open this file in any spreadsheet software like Microsoft Excel or Google Sheets.
  • Q: What should I do if I cannot see the results?
  • A: Ensure that both the department and semester dropdowns have been selected. If the issue persists, please contact the system administrator.

If you have any further questions, feel free to contact support.
